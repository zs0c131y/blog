About Me

Professional Educator & Counselor

Bridging urban-rural education gaps through value-based learning

I am a finance graduate with a diploma in counseling skills, working as a freelance life skills trainer. I hold certifications in child and adolescent behavior and serve as a professional counselor.

For nearly a decade, I have worked as a trainer in the NIE (Newspaper in Education) division of The Times Group, where I have conducted over 300 workshops to date. My specialization includes Indian traditional games and designing theme-based educational activities.

I actively engage in volunteer work with various social service organizations and am currently working with FTS Ekal Abhiyan to bridge the educational gap between urban and rural communities through free, value-based education programs.
